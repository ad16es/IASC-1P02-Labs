Things to see in Spain:


There are many beautiful things to see in Spain. It is full of castles, chruches, old libraries, etc. Some notable places are:

  • Sagrada Família: located in Barcelona, the construction of this building dates back to 1882. The original architect passed away in 1926, so construction stopped, but its projected completion year is in 2026.
  • Basílica de Santa María la Real de Covadonga, Parque Nacional de Los Picos de Europa, Asturias: This chruch is contructed of pink limestone, giving the historical Neo-Romanesque chruch a pink hue.
  • The Real Alcázar: located in Seville, Alcázar has massive gardens with fountains, trees, flowers etc.
  • El Tajo Gorge: located in one of Spain's oldest towns, Ronda, built back in the 9th century. Overlooking beautiful mountaintop views.
  • Catedral de Santiago de Compostela: located in Santiago, is a very important place to those who practice Roman Catholicism, as this is the burial spot of St. James, one the the 12 apostles.
