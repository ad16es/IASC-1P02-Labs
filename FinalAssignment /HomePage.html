Spain, or España, is a European Country in Southwestern Europe, specifically the Iberian Peninsula. Spain is offically called, Reino de España, which translates to "Kingdom on Spain" It's coast touch two bodies of water, the Mediterranean Sea on the East, and the Atlantic Ocean on the West. Spain's population is made up of 47 million people making it the fourth largest country in the European continent. (“Spain.” Wikipedia, Wikimedia Foundation, 1 Dec. 2019.)
